Exploratory Data Analysis Project

EDA - HoneyBees

This project was an opportunity to gain experience in exploratory data analysis to better understand a dataset through investigative questions and prelimary insights and hypotheses. Phase 1 consisted of understanding the shape and structure of the dataset by reviewing variables, distributions, and noting possible quality issues. Phase 2 consisted of presenting visualizatios and summaries that highlight trends and patterns to provide an informative analysis of honey bee colony losses.
