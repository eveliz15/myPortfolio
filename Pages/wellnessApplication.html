Wellness Web App Project

Wellness Web Application

This survey-based application takes a hollistic approach to promoting health and wellness by providing users with tools for personal growth within the 8 dimensions of wellness. Users are able to create a profile and establish how important certain areas of wellness are. They can then take a survey to track how fullfilled these areas of wellness were for the day and add an activity they may have done. Once the data is saved, users can compare their data and answer the question "how are you doing?" by reviewing their personal radar charts. The front-end is built with React, JavaScript, and Bootstrap while the back-end uses Java, SpringBoot and is hosted through Heroku with a PostgresQL database.


This project was a great opportunity for gaining real-world software development experience. While there were challenges along the way, I took part in learning new tools - GIT, Jira, Flyway, and more - engaged in a range of roles from project management to database design, and developed a wide range of new skills to ensure my ability to develop software.
